Usage guidance


  • Use SVGs: keep edges sharp, especially on modern displays.
  • Preserve spacing: don’t crowd the mark against borders, headlines, or photo edges.
  • Don’t recreate: avoid redrawing, outlining, stretching, or adding effects.
  • Prefer real UI: use official screenshots instead of mockups that drift from reality.
