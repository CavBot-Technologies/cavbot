CavBot FAQ preview

  • How does CavBot choose what to watch first? ▾
    CavBot starts with routes you label as high-value: checkouts, sign-ups, campaign landings, and flows with real cost. You choose the journeys; CavBot tracks 404s, structure, and runtime feel.
  • What makes a 404 “critical”? ▾
    A critical 404 is one that breaks a primary journey (revenue, onboarding, or key navigation) or appears as a new spike from meaningful referrers. Severity comes from context, not just volume.
  • Where do invoices and receipts go? ▾
    Route finance requests through the Billing lane. Include org/workspace name and the invoice ID if you have it.
  • How do I report an incident? ▾
    Use the Incident lane: route + timestamps + what changed + reproduction steps. Keep it short and measurable.
  • How do privacy requests work? ▾
    Include jurisdiction (GDPR/CCPA), org name, and the exact request (export/deletion). Use the Privacy lane in the contact map.
