CavBot Arcade FAQ

Simple answers to common questions.





What is the CavBot Arcade?

CavBot Arcade exists to reframe failure as opportunity. A broken route isn't an error to hide - it's a moment to re-engage. By transforming 404s into calm, intentional interactive experiences, CavBot Arcade keeps visitors present, restores momentum, and turns dead ends into meaningful signal.

Why games on a 404 product?

Because a 404 is a moment of attention. Someone arrived with intent - and the path disappeared. Games turn that pause into engagement, restore momentum, and give visitors a reason to continue instead of leave. A 404 doesn't need an apology. It needs a better experience.

Are these games playable on mobile?

Yes. Every game is built to work seamlessly across phone, tablet, and desktop - adapting naturally to each screen without compromise.

Does sound matter?

Sound is optional. All games are fully playable without audio, with sound designed as a subtle enhancement - not a requirement.
